Singapore Trip Plan

June 17 2026

Hi, In this blog post i will tell you the plan of 'The OG team' to vist Singapore.

The Start

On July 30th, around 7 am. We are supposed to land on The ' Singapore Changi Airport (SIN) '
is not just an airport. It’s basically a full entertainment + transit mega-hub that also happens to handle one of the world’s busiest air traffic flows. It is ranked World's Best Airport in the 2026 Skytrax World Airport Awards.

Moving forward, The Airport has 4 terminals

  • Terminal 1

    • One of the oldest, recently modernized
    Direct connection to Jewel
    Handles many international flights

  • Terminal 2

    • Recently renovated and reopened
    Heavy focus on comfort + retail upgrades

  • Terminal 3

    • One of the most “premium feel” terminals
    Connected to Jewel via skybridge
    Big lounges, long-haul flights

  • Terminal 4

    • Fully automated terminal
    Self check-in, bag drop, immigration using biometrics
    No direct Skytrain to other terminals (shuttle bus instead)

After Landing, Walk / Skytrain to immigration. Yes, there is a train in the station. The Changi Airport Skytrain is completely free to use. It connects Terminals 1, 2, and 3 in both the transit (airside) and public (landside) areas. Operating Hours: 5:00 AM to 2:00 AM daily.
Singapore immigration is fast (usually 15–40 min). Collect baggage if checked-in. One thing to note, Don’t rush. Changi is designed to slow you down after clearance, not before. until you are ready and good to go. 1 hour will be passed. (Around 8 am)

After immigration, Go directly to Jewel Changi Airport (linked to T1, T2, T3)

What to do here ?

  • Rain Vortex (main waterfall) → go center level first

  • Forest Valley walk (upper floors for better view), Its free. 100% reccomended to visit

  • Canopy Bridge (paid, if you want views from above)

  • Quick walk through main atrium + shops

Don't forget to take plenty of photos at the Rain Vortex and Forest Valley

There are some paid things to explore in the airport. I'll mention some here.

1. Canopy Park Entry (Base ticket)

~ S$5 – S$8 (basic entry range)

What you get:

  • Foggy Bowls (misty walk zones)

  • Topiary Walk (cute shaped plants)

  • Discovery Slides (small aesthetic slides)

  • Chill walking garden space

This is a place where you are supposed to take pictures. Its not a place for adventure. 100% reccomended if you want some cool pictures

  • 3. Mirror Maze (Most aesthetic + confusing)
